About Us

Welcome to Banana Leaf, where we bring the vibrant and flavorful cuisine of Sri Lanka to your table. Our mission is to provide our guests with an authentic dining experience that celebrates the rich culinary heritage of our homeland.

Founded in 2020, Banana Leaf is a family-owned restaurant dedicated to serving traditional Sri Lankan dishes made from the freshest ingredients. Our chefs use time-honored recipes and cooking techniques to create meals that are both delicious and memorable.

Our Team

Our team is a diverse group of passionate individuals who share a love for great food and hospitality. From our chefs in the kitchen to our servers in the dining room, each member of our team is committed to making your visit to Banana Leaf a delightful experience.

Our Mission and Values

At Banana Leaf, we believe in the power of good food to bring people together. Our mission is to share the rich flavors and vibrant culture of Sri Lanka with our community, creating a welcoming space where everyone can enjoy a delicious meal.

We are committed to:

  • Using the freshest and highest quality ingredients
  • Providing exceptional service to our guests
  • Celebrating the culinary traditions of Sri Lanka
  • Creating a warm and inviting atmosphere for all
